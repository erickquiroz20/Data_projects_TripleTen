# Restaurant Performance Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes restaurant, menu, and order data to identify business trends, pricing patterns, and revenue opportunities.

The goal was to better understand how restaurant pricing segments and sales performance differ across the dataset.

## Business Problem
Restaurants need to understand how pricing, product mix, and order activity affect revenue performance. This analysis helps identify which segments generate the most sales and where business improvements may be needed.

## Tools Used
- Excel / Google Sheets
- Tableau

## Process
- Cleaned and reviewed restaurant, menu, and order datasets
- Combined data sources for analysis
- Evaluated restaurant pricing using cost-based segmentation
- Built visualizations to analyze sales distribution and category-level performance
- Interpreted trends and business patterns from the dashboard

## Key Findings
-Revenue is concentrated among a small group of top-performing restaurants
- Cuisine diversity, particularly combinations of popular cuisines, appears to drive strong revenue performance
- High ratings alone do not guarantee financial success
- Mid-range and affordable restaurants generate the majority of platform revenue, suggesting that price accessibility plays a major role in customer ordering behavior
- Some categories showed weaker performance, indicating opportunities for improvement
- Clear data visualization made it easier to identify sales concentration and underperforming segments

## Business Recommendation
- Promote multi-cuisine restaurants to maximize revenue potential 
- Promote high-performing restaurants and brands on the platform to further leverage their strong demand
- Focus marketing on mid-range price restaurants, which currently generate the largest share of revenue
